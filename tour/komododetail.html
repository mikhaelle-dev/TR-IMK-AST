Travel Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Tanah Lot Sunset

Pick up from Ngurah Rai International Airport, enjoy a local lunch, and witness the iconic sunset at Tanah Lot Temple.

Day 2

Nusa Penida Day Trip

Cross to Nusa Penida by fast boat. Visit Kelingking Beach and Broken Beach, then enjoy snorkeling at Crystal Bay.

Day 3

Ubud Culture & Tegalalang

Explore the stunning rice terraces of Tegalalang Rice Terrace, experience the famous Bali Swing, and visit Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary and the Ubud Art Market.

Day 4

Shopping & Departure

Shop for Balinese souvenirs at Krisna or Erlangga, enjoy a relaxing lunch in Jimbaran, and transfer back to the airport for your departure.
